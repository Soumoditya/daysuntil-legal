Privacy Policy for Days Until - Countdown Widget

Last updated: 2 July 2026

Days Until - Countdown Widget ("the app") does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data or usage information of any kind. The app does not require or request an internet connection - it does not declare the Android internet permission, so it has no technical ability to send data anywhere, even if it wanted to. There are no advertisements, no analytics or tracking software development kits, no user accounts, no sign-up or login of any kind, and no third-party services embedded in the app. All countdowns, settings, reminders, and preferences you create are stored exclusively in local storage on your own device and are never transmitted off of it; if you uninstall the app, that data is deleted along with it. The only data the app touches beyond local storage is handled entirely by Google Play's own systems when you choose to make the optional one-time "Premium Themes" purchase, which is processed by Google Play Billing under Google's own privacy policy and is never seen by the app developer beyond confirmation that a purchase was made. If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact the developer at soumodityapramanik@gmail.com.
